About CannaBiz HR

Bridging municipal government experience with cannabis industry innovation

Our Story

CannaBiz HR was founded on the belief that the cannabis industry deserves professional HR services that understand both the complexities of regulatory compliance and the innovative spirit of this emerging sector.

With a Master of Public Administration (MPA) degree and extensive experience in municipal government human resources, our founder brings a unique perspective to cannabis industry consulting. This background provides deep understanding of regulatory frameworks, compliance requirements, and the intersection between public policy and business operations.

Our journey into cannabis HR consulting began with recognizing the need for professional services that could navigate the complex regulatory landscape while supporting business growth and innovation. The combination of municipal government experience, advanced technology skills, and passion for the cannabis industry creates a unique value proposition for our clients.

Based in Los Angeles County, we understand the local business environment and have established relationships within the municipal government structure that benefit our clients' compliance and operational needs.
