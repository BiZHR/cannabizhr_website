Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cannabis HR consulting services

What makes CannaBiz HR different from other HR consultants?

Our unique combination of municipal government experience, MPA degree, and cannabis industry expertise provides unparalleled insights into regulatory compliance and best practices. We understand both the regulatory expectations and the practical challenges of cannabis businesses.

Do you really offer free consultations?

Yes! We provide genuine 30-minute consultations at no cost to help cannabis businesses understand their options and get initial guidance. There's no obligation to continue with paid services.

What areas of Los Angeles County do you serve?

We serve all of Los Angeles County and can provide services throughout California via remote consultations. Our local expertise is particularly valuable for businesses dealing with LA County-specific regulations.

Can you help with cannabis license applications?

Absolutely. Our municipal government background provides unique insights into the application process, and we can help with HR-related components of license applications, renewals, and compliance maintenance.

Do you provide ongoing support or just one-time consulting?

We offer both options. Many clients start with project-based consulting and then move to ongoing retainer relationships for continued compliance support and HR guidance as their business grows.

What technology solutions do you provide?

We develop custom web applications, implement HRIS systems, create compliance tracking tools, and provide data analysis solutions tailored specifically for cannabis industry needs and regulatory requirements.
